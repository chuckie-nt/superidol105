Examples Of Streaming Platforms

Netflix

Netflix is a subscription-based streaming service that allows multiple accounts to access the available tv shows and movies without any commercials on an internet-connected device. You can also download tv shows in the app onto your iOS, Android, or Windows 10 device and watch without the need for an internet connection.

Hulu

Hulu is the leading premium streaming service offering live and on-demand TV and movies, with and without commercials, both in and outside the home.

Amazon Prime Video

Prime Video delivers Amazon Originals and other exclusives, popular titles included with Prime, plus premium channel subscriptions, titles available to rent or buy, live events and more.
